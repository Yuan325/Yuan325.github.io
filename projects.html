Neutral News

This project is built within 24 hours of DubHacks 2020 with 4 other team member: Ryan, Calista, Haardik, and Owais.
Neutral news is a chrome extension that automatically searches for related news post in your Facebook feed and adds articles to help keeping your feed "balanced".
We utilized Google Cloud's Natural Language Processing, Google Custom Search, and DynamoDB.
